About Smart Baby Skin Scanner

Smart Baby Skin Scanner is an AI-powered medical assistance tool designed to help parents and doctors identify early symptoms of baby skin conditions. Using advanced deep learning models, our system analyzes uploaded images and provides fast and accurate predictions to support early diagnosis.

How It Works

1. Upload Image

Parents or doctors upload a clear image of the baby's affected skin area.

2. AI Detection

The deep-learning model analyzes the skin and detects possible conditions like rashes, eczema, infections, etc.

3. Instant Result

The system displays the predicted condition along with confidence score.

4. Consult Doctor

Parents can chat with doctors or schedule online consultations.
